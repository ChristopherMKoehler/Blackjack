Instructions

Rules of the game
  1. Place your bet using the buttons on the bottom right. Press a button multiple times to add that amount multiple times to your current bet.
  2. The options on the current hand (hit, stand, split, double down) will be given on the bottom right. Click one to make your move.
  3. Once you stand, bust, or hit blackjack, the dealer will make his move and the app will decide who won and will update your chips accordingly.
  4. Press play again to restart the game. If you ran out of chips, you will have your chips reset to 2,000.
